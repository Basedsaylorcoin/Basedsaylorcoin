Total Supply: 21000000 Based Saylor Fun paired with 0.00004500 Coinbase Wrapped Bitcoin on Base Blockchain

Until the first 2100 buy transactions are completed no address can hold more than 5000 BSLR tokens. This means that a single address can buy at most 5k tokens during this time. After that the next 210 buy transactions have a limit of 50k BSLR per address. After that no limits apply and BSLR becomes a standard token. Also selling is disabled until 2310 buy transactions pass.
